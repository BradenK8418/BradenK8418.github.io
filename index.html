The Arts Emergency Manifesto

There are things that are bigger and better than money.

We have things that those in power will never understand.

Be fearless.

You are just as entitled as anyone to have the life you want.

The future is another place. Allow yourself to believe a better life is possible.

Say it loud, defiantly, to everyone.

To think creatively and critically is to free yourself from the limits of circumstance, class and context.
